About me

Welcome to my home page. My name is Henna and I am a coder from Finland, currently living in Germany. My current interests lie in mainly Web Development and Software Development. The languages I am using are CSS, HTML, JavaScript and Python.

I am self taught coder and have been coding/teaching myself since the beginning of February 2020. Beginning from 28.9.2020 I am participating in the Neuefische Web Development Bootcamp located in Munich to strengthen and deepen the skills I have learnt on my own. Beside coding when I have the time I enjoy hiking up in the beautiful Alps, which are right next door to where I live. When not out in nature (sweating) I enjoy my coffee breaks and reading a really good fantasy book.

If you got interested in knowing more about what I have to offer and done before choosing to enter the coding path, please keep scrolling!
